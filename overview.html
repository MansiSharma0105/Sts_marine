Overview

With an overarching focus on crew and vessel safety, and care for the environment, STS Marine Management provides a broad range of technical management and related services to ship-owners globally.

At the core of our business, the portfolio of vessels entrusted to us is managed by a highly-experienced, professional onshore team headquartered in Mumbai, India and comprised of ex-Masters and Chief Engineers, together with very experienced personnel and purchasing managers. Our approach to vessel management is simple and effective: by managing ships as if they were our own, we are able to give owners assurance regarding the maintenance status and cost-efficient operation of their vessels, and support that with regular, transparent financial and operational reporting.

  • For many types of cost, we are able to generate savings that we can pass on to owners to further enhance the value of the services we provide.
  • We provide owners with a range of other specific vessel-related services, including marine superintendence, insurance placement, project supervision, and in-house managing that makes use of our dedicated crew selection and recruitment centre.
  • What every our technical requirements, please contact us and we would be pleased to tailor a solution that meets your needs.
