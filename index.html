About STS Marine Management
Provider of high-quality, cost-efficient, and transparent technical management services to ship-owners and financiers. STS Marine Management is a fully integrated provider of shipping management services and commercial management company.

As an experienced operator, we provide high-quality technical management to customers globally with a uniquely broad range of vessels and the largest member base—our pools span clean tankers, chemical, and crude oil tankers.

The flexibility we offer has enabled us to create a network of relationships with major oil companies. Advanced IT systems and a market-leading team help optimize operations, improve decision-making, and drive cost efficiencies—consistently delivering superior service and performance.

Overview

With an overarching focus on crew and vessel safety, and care for the environment, STS Marine Management provides a broad range of technical management and related services to ship-owners globally.

At the core of our business, the portfolio of vessels entrusted to us is managed by a highly-experienced, professional onshore team headquartered in Mumbai, India and comprised of ex-Masters and Chief Engineers, together with very experienced personnel and purchasing managers. Our approach to vessel management is simple and effective: by managing ships as if they were our own, we are able to give owners assurance regarding the maintenance status and cost-efficient operation of their vessels, and support that with regular, transparent financial and operational reporting.

  • For many types of cost, we are able to generate savings that we can pass on to owners to further enhance the value of the services we provide.
  • We provide owners with a range of other specific vessel-related services, including marine superintendence, insurance placement, project supervision, and in-house managing that makes use of our dedicated crew selection and recruitment centre.

Technical Management

Combining a highly-experienced onshore team and selected vessel crews, STS Marine Management delivers high-quality technical management services to shipowners worldwide.

Our approach is built on prevention, safety, and operational excellence. We ensure round-the-clock availability of skilled personnel to manage events effectively and efficiently.

We provide transparent, user-friendly reports detailing maintenance status, technical plans, and cost analysis. Contact us to discuss how we can support your technical needs.

Marine Superintendence

Our team of ex-Chief Engineers and ex-Masters provides reliable marine superintendence services with deep knowledge of class, flag, and compliance protocols.

With technical expertise and cost-conscious planning, we handle maintenance and repairs with attention to detail and maximum efficiency.

We supervise crews, monitor equipment, and enhance performance through practical procedural improvements. Reach out to explore project-based or full-management services.

Whether you need Fleet Entry or Individual Placement, we ensure your vessel is well protected and compliant with industry standards.

Let us quote your vessel — we're happy to assist with customized insurance solutions.

Services

Technical Management

Combining a highly-experienced, professional onshore team and selected vessel crews, STS Marine Management provides high-quality technical management services to ship-owners globally.
Our approach to technical management is founded on the principle that prevention is better thancure. Focusing our efforts on maintaining the safety of our vessels and crews, we minimize operational and environmental risk, and ensure the constant availability of experienced personnel to manage unforeseen events.
As with any investment, we understand the desire of owners for regular updates on the physical,operational and financial condition of their assets. We provide tailored, transparent and user-friendly vessel reporting to ship-owners that detail vessel maintenance status, purchasing and technical maintenance plans and history,and comprehensive costs analysis.
Please contact us to discuss your technical management requirements and how we can fulfill them.

Marine Superintendence

With a very experienced team of ex-Chief Engineers and ex-Masters,STS Marine Management Pvt. Ltd.offers high-quality technical and marine superintendence services.
Fully versed in class, flag and regulatory compliance requirements, our Technical Superintendents are dedicated to specific vessels to ensure greatest familiarity with the systems aboard the ships under their care. With long experience in handling the planning and logistics of all technical maintenance and repair activities,you can be assured of the utmost attention to detail,and a keen focus on costs management to deliver maximum value.
With direct experience of vessel operational and crew management, our Marine Superintendents are ideally placed to ensure the maintenance and replacement of vessel equipment,supervision of vessel personnel,and propose procedural improvements to deliver enhanced vessel performance.
Whether as part of a wider vessel management mandate,or on a project basis,please contact us to discuss how we can support the optimal,cost-effective operation of your vessel.

Insurance Placement

Primarily provided through our Mumbai & Lagos office, we provide comprehensive insurance placement services, covering all that a ship owner may need to fully insure a vessel. We can offer hull & machinery(H&M),and (whererequired) loss of hire,placement in the London markets,and can place protection & indemnity (P&I) cover with leading insurer, American Club. Placement can be made under a Fleet Entry or Individual Placement basis,as required.
Please contact us to discuss a vessel insurance quotation and we would be pleased to assist you.
